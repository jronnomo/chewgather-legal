# Chewgather — Support

Chewgather is a social dining app: swipe through nearby restaurants, plan meals, and let your group vote on where to eat. The end of the dinner group chat.

## Get help

For any issue — bugs, account problems, questions, feedback — email us and we'll get back to you quickly:

**ggronnii@gmail.com**

## Common questions

**How do I delete my account?**
In the app: Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and removes your account and all of your data (plans, friendships, votes, notifications, photo).

**How do I report a user or a plan?**
Open the plan and choose *Report Plan* or *Block Organizer* from the actions menu, or long-press a friend to block them. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

**How do I manage blocked users?**
Profile → Blocked Users — you can unblock anyone there.

**Why does the app ask for my location?**
Only to find restaurants near you while the app is open. You can enter a zip code instead — location is never required and never tracked in the background.
